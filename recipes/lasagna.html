How to make Lasagna

Lasagna is very time consuming but it is well worth the wait.Even Garfield can't stop his love for lasagna anyways, lets get started below will be the ingredients needed for this classic

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and Garlic
  • Tomato
  • Sugar
  • Herbs and Spices
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving
