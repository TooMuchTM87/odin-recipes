Affogato Easy steps

Affogato is just espresso and ice cream mixed together, who would've thought ice cream and coffee go so well together

Ingredients

  • 5 1/2 ounces vanilla gelato
  • 2 ounces fresh brewed espresso
    •

    Steps

  • place gelato in glass and pour espresso over the top. Serve immediately
