How To Make Marinated Flank Steak

Flank steak is best prepared marinated because its a cut from the lower abdominal of the cow and can be cooked three ways the grill,oven or the stove

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 1/3 cup low-sodium soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup red wine vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons worcestershire sauce
  • 1 tablespoon dijon mustard
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • steak 1lb

Steps

  1. gather all ingredients
  2. Whisk the oil, soy sauce, vinegar, lemon juice, worcestershire sauce, dijon mustard, garlic and pepper for marinade until thoroughly combined
  3. pour the marinade over the flank in a baking dish; turn several times to coat thoroughly. Cover and refigerate up to 2 to 6 hours or 12 if possible
  4. when ready to cook preheat the grill for medium or high heat and lighly oil the grate
  5. remove steak from marinade and shake off the excess
  6. cook steak 5 min per side, or to desired doneness
  7. remove from the grill and let rest for 5 min
  8. serve hot and enjoy
